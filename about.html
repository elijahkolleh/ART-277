About Us

Smart Kids Kindergarden

Smart Kids Kindergarden school is located at Airfield. We help parents that are so busy to guide and teach their low age children. The purpose of this school is to provide your child with an opportunity to learn and practice the essential social, emotional, problem-solving, and study skills that he will use throughout his schooling. The development of self-esteem is one of the important goals of kindergarten.
